Biryani

Here is the recipes of Biryani:

Description

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups ater
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 Cloves
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1-inch Cinnamon Stick
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 500 g chicken
  • Half cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tap garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 2 onions
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1 tsp biryani masala

Steps

  1. Prepare the Rice
  2. Marinate the Meat (or Vegetables)
  3. Fry the Onions (For Biryani)
  4. Prepare the Biryani Gravy
  5. Layer the Biryani
  6. Final Cooking (Dum)
  7. Serve
